Petuk Account Deletion Request

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Petuk mobile app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  3. Confirm your phone number
  4. Enter the OTP received via SMS
  5. Tap "Permanently Delete Account"

Alternatively, email us at taahir.info@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request" from your registered email.

What Data We Delete

  • Your profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Saved delivery addresses
  • Order history
  • Phone number (from our main database)

What We Retain

  • Transaction records (for legal/financial compliance) - 5 years
  • Phone number hash (for fraud prevention) - 1 year
  • App activity logs (anonymized) - 30 days

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation SMS when completed.

Note: This action is irreversible. You'll need to create a new account to use Petuk services again.
